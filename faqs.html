BIS (Board of International Scholarships) is Pakistan's premier, internationally recognized scholarship authority, established in September 2004. Our mission is to elevate educational opportunities beyond borders by providing merit & need base financial support, fostering academic excellence, and empowering students to achieve their full potential without the burden of financial constraints. Through strategic partnerships and innovative programs, we aim to bridge global educational gaps and cultivate future leaders who will drive positive change in Pakistan and beyond.

BIS maintains a fair, transparent selection process through a structured merit-need balanced system. Applications undergo anonymized review by independent experts assessing academic performance (CGPA, test scores, research), while financial need is verified through income/asset audits. Multi-tier committees (academia, industry, civil society) evaluate candidates using predefined 70% merit / 30% need criteria, with rubrics publicly disclosed. The blockchain-secured process includes public result declarations and grievance redressal, maintaining 100% audit compliance with HEC/ISO standards to ensure equal opportunity for all qualified applicants.

The BIS scholarship provides full financial coverage including tuition fees, university charges, living expenses (accommodation and three daily meals), health insurance, and round-trip airfare. Selected international students also receive visa assistance. This comprehensive support allows scholars to focus entirely on their academic goals without financial constraints.

The application process for the BIS scholarship involves several key steps:

  1. Online Application: Submit the application form through the BIS website.
  2. Document Submission: Provide required documents, including academic transcripts, CV, and recommendation letters.
  3. Application Fee: Verified candidates must pay small non-refundable application Fee 10$ via any banking branches.
  4. Interview: Shortlisted candidates may be invited for an interview.
  5. Final Selection: Successful candidates will receive an official scholarship offer.

Verified applicants have to pay their non-refundable Applicants Fee. After this you will got email about your online interview within 30 working days. Email contain following details:

  • Your interview date & time
  • Interview link of Zoom
  • A basic PDF of precations and questions

The BIS scholarship may be revoked under certain circumstances, including:

  • Academic Performance: Recipients must maintain a minimum 70% GPA as specified in their scholarship agreement.
  • Misconduct: Any form of academic dishonesty or misconduct may result in revocation.
  • Change in Financial Status: Significant changes in a recipient's financial situation must be reported and may affect scholarship status.
